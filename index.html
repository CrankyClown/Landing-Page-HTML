FAQ's

What are Forever Frog NFTs?

Forever Frog NFTs are unique ERC-721 tokens created on the Ethereum blockchain, that bring multi-utility benefits to their owners. The Forever Frog NFT collection is a set of 7,777 hand drawn pieces, with randomly generated attributes, which grant the holders the ability to stake $FFROG

How many Forever Frog NFTs can we mint?

All Whitelisted members will be able to mint a maximum of 2 NFT's during the 90-minute WL pre-sale period. All Public minters will be able to mint a maximum of 10 NFT's

How does the Community Wallet work?

The community wallet will be continually topped up using 20% of all profits from the secondary market royalties. This community wallet will be used to give Forever Frog NFT holders amazing giveaways and prizes, all year round.

Can I resell my NFT's?

Yes, Forever Frog NFTs will be publicly traded on different marketplaces, such as Opensea, Rarible, LooksRare, etc

When is mint day and is there a Whitelist allocation?

We are still to confirm the official mint date, but YES! There will be a very small whitelist allocation of 150 WL spots. We want to keep our whitelist very exclusive, and only for the people who really deserve it most. Whitelisted members will get 90 minutes to mint at the reduced price.

What benefits do Forever Frog NFT holders get?

Holders of Forever Frog NFTs with get the following benefits: 1. Ability to stake their NFT and receive exclusive rewards 2. Access to the Forever Frog metaverse 3. Free & Exclusive entries into HUGE giveaways, to win Ethereum airdrops, PS5 bundles, Oculus sets, Yeezys and more. 4. Ability to generate $FFROG – a unique internal currency, with which holder can use in multiple ways.
